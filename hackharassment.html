The HackHarassment Challenge is a competition dedicated to making technological solutions to combat online harassment.

Every Hackital team can take part in the challenge: creating a tech tool to help make the Internet a more inclusive place. The top 5 teams with the coolest products will win prizes. Competing teams will have access to online harassment and cyberbullying datasets, statistics, and articles, along with informational workshops.

Sponsored by Hack Harassment, Intel, the Born This Way Foundation, and the Silicon Valley Community Foundation.

We are an MLH member event and stand for inclusivity. See our Code of Conduct.
